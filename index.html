About

My work with aerial photography began in 2017 with my first drone purchase, the DJI Spark. My interest was "sparked" rather quickly for capturing landscapes unseen by the human eye.


In 2023, I purchased the DJI Mini 4 Pro in anticipation of a four month program in Europe. This drone was a massive technological and photographic advancement to my preceding drone. With this, I was able to capture memories from all of my travels across seven countries.


Then in October 2024, I acquired the most advanced prosumer drone on the market available right now - the DJI Air 3S. With this drone I have been able to break aerial barriers. Most noticeably, a novel feature found on the Air 3S called free panoramic. You will find these photos in the Colorado collection and every collection since 2025.


My passion for aerial photography & videography was taken a step further when I acquired my FAA Part 107 drone license in November 2024. With this license, I am authorized by the FAA to operate my fleet of drones for commercial applications.


Going into 2025, I am exploring commercial use cases, such as real estate, golf courses, weddings, car meets, and much more.


Any inquiries to discuss partnering with me to capture photography or videography can be sent via the contact page.
